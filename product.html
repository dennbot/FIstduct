FirstDuct's R-Value Index

The R Value is a measure of thermal resistanceused in the building and construction industry. It is a unit for a particular material or assembly of materials and it is also expressed as the thickness of the material normalized to the thermal conductivity. The higher number, the better the building insulation's theoretical effectiveness.
