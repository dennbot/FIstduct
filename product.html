Pre Insulated Panels

Through years of contribution in air-conditioning industry, FirstDuct has given high quality air-duct panels with smooth or embossed finishing panels. FirstDuct's panels are using Poly Isocyanurate Rigid Form (PIR) with better fire retardancy, higher density, and better R Value.
